Learn more about Harry Potter: Wizards Unite!

Harry Potter: Wizards Unite is the real-world augmented reality (AR) game inspired by the Wizarding World that puts magic in the hands of players worldwide. Harry Potter: Wizards Unite is co-developed and co-published by Niantic. A calamity has befallen the wizarding world, causing Foundables—magical artefacts, creatures, people, and even memories—to mysteriously appear in the Muggle world. Here, we will help you to know the characteristics of the characters and their spells to use them to your advantage y protect the wizarding world from a global calamit.

Wands and Characters

A wand is the object through which a witch or wizard channels her magic. It is made of wood and has a magical substance at its center. The wands made by Ollivander have phoenix feather cores, unicorn tail hair or dragon heart fiber, and they are of different woods, lengths and flexibilities

¿What is a patronus?

It is a positive force, a projection of the very things that the Dementor feeds on: hope, happiness, the desire to survive, but he cannot feel despair, like a real human, so the Dementors cannot fight against it.
